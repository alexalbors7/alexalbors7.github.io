Transformer Pix2Pix

I trained a transformer-based Pix2Pix GAN as a final project for CSE493G1.

We trained introduced transformers (Vaswani et al., 2017) into a standard Pix2Pix GAN (Isola et al., 2017), and experimented with four different, novel architectures. The final project report, incwhich includes all output images, a detailed implementation, and training curves, can be found here
