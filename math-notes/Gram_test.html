Given vectors \( v_1, v_2, \dots, v_n \), the Gram-Schmidt process produces an orthonormal set \( u_1, u_2, \dots, u_n \) such that \[ u_k = \frac{v_k - \sum_{j=1}^{k-1} \langle v_k, u_j \rangle u_j}{\| v_k - \sum_{j=1}^{k-1} \langle v_k, u_j \rangle u_j \|}. \] and also consider \begin{align*} \frac{v}{u} = \int_{u}^{v} \hspace{2pt} u dt \end{align*}
